Lists

What are list?

As the name itself tell, list are data which is not written as paragraphs but in a structured way, like next thing occupy next line when the former one is finished.

List are of two types: ordered and unordered

for ordered we use ol and for unordered we use ul

Ordered List

  1. 3
  2. 2
  3. 1

Unordered List

  • 3
  • 2
  • 1

As you can see, the ordered list displays items in a sequence, while the unordered list does not have a specific order. Despite, they both are written in same manner.
